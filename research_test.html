An integral comparison of crystalline and de Rham cohomology

Collaborators: Abhinandan

Status: Submitted

\(p\)-adic Hodge theory prismatic \(F\)-gauges crystalline de Rham
Description

We give an integral comparison between twisted crystalline and twisted de Rham cohomology, using a Dwork-style argument in the stacky approach to prismatic cohomology, allowing arbitrary prismatic coefficients. For the structure sheaf this recovers (rationally) the Berthelot–Ogus isomorphism and clarifies the relationship between torsion in crystalline and de Rham cohomology.

An integral analogue of Fontaine's crystalline functor

Collaborators: Naoki Imai and Hiroki Kato

Status: Submitted

\(p\)-adic Hodge theory prismatic \(F\)-gauges Fontaine–Laffaille
Description

We develop an integral analogue of \(D_{\mathrm{crys}}\) and relate it to Fontaine–Laffaille and Dieudonné theory. In the Fontaine–Laffaille range \([0,p-2]\), local systems, Fontaine–Laffaille modules, prismatic \(F\)-crystals, and prismatic \(F\)-gauges coincide, explaining both the successes within the range and failures outside it.

Beauville—Laszlo gluing of algebraic spaces

Collaborators: Piotr Achinger

Status: Submitted

algebraic spaces rigid geometry
Description

We show a Beauville–Laszlo type gluing theorem for algebraic spaces over \(\mathbb{Z}_p\) (and more general bases), equating gluing triples \((X,\mathfrak{X},j)\) with separated algebraic spaces and explaining several phenomena in arithmetic geometry.

Canonical integral models of Shimura varieties of abelian type

Collaborators: Patrick Daniels

Status: Forum of Mathematics, Sigma

Shimura varieties integral models \(p\)-adic Hodge theory \(p\)-adic shtukas
Description

We prove the Pappas–Rapoport conjecture for abelian-type Shimura varieties with parahoric level \(p>3\), showing Kisin–Pappas–Zhou integral models are canonical and functorial in the Shimura datum.

Collaborators: Naoki Imai and Hiroki Kato

Status: Submitted

Shimura varieties \(p\)-adic Hodge theory prismatic \(F\)-gauges Fontaine–Laffaille
Description

We construct a prismatic \(F\)-gauge realization on integral models of abelian-type Shimura varieties (hyperspecial level), yielding new \(p\)-adic Hodge-theoretic information (e.g. a Serre–Tate-type statement) and characterizations even at finite levels.

The Jacobson–Morozov morphism for Langlands parameters in the relative setting

Collaborators: Alexander Bertoloni Meli and Naoki Imai

Status: International Mathematics Research Notices

Langlands \(L\)-parameters moduli
Description

We construct a moduli space of \(L\)-parameters over \(\mathbb{Q}\), prove smoothness and a description of connected components, and exhibit a morphism to the moduli of Weil–Deligne parameters that is an isomorphism over a dense open subset.

Specialization for the pro-étale fundamental group

Collaborators: Piotr Achinger and Marcin Lara

Status: Compositio Mathematica

rigid geometry covering spaces fundamental groups
Description

For a formal scheme \(\mathfrak{X}\) over a height-1 valuation ring, we construct a specialization homomorphism \(\pi_1^\mathrm{dJ}(\mathfrak{X}_\eta)\to \pi_1^\mathrm{proet}(\mathfrak{X}_s)\), prove surjectivity in good cases, and show de Jong coverings are étale-local on the target for smooth quasi-compact rigid spaces over an equicharacteristic-0 non-archimedean field.

Geometric arcs and fundamental groups of rigid spaces

Collaborators: Piotr Achinger and Marcin Lara

Status: Journal für die reine und angewandte Mathematik

geometric coverings rigid geometry fundamental groups
Description

We define geometric coverings in rigid geometry (adapting Bhatt–Scholze’s notion) and show stability under composition and disjoint union, étale locality on the target, and that the category forms a tame infinite Galois category supporting a fundamental group.

Description

Using the geometric covering framework we address questions of de Jong, compare admissible/étale local notions, and identify \(\mathbf{Loc}(X_{\mathrm{proet}})\) with \(\mathbf{Cov}^\mathrm{et}_X\), making the former essentially a tame infinite Galois category.
