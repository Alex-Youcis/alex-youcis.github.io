Special Session: Shimura Varieties & Arithmetic
AMS Spring Sectional — Boston College, Mar 28–29, 2026

General information

Full meeting information: AMS sectional meeting page.

Location & time: Boston College (Chestnut Hill, MA). The session runs Sat–Sun, Mar 28–29, 2026, with talks in the following blocks: Saturday 8:00–11:00 and 15:00–18:00; Sunday 8:00–11:00 and 14:00–17:00.

Brief description: A special session on Shimura varieties and related aspects of arithmetic geometry, automorphic forms, and \(p\)-adic/cohomological methods.

Travel funding: Graduate-student travel support information: AMS travel grants .
