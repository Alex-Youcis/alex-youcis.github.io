About Me

My name is Alex Youcis (the IPA transcription for my last name is juːkɪs). Currently, I am a postdoc at the National University of Singapore.

My Research Interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests are arithmetic geometry, rigid geometry, and \(p\)-adic Hodge theory. I am particularly interested in interactions of these topics with the Langlands program.
